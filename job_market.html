    2. Interviews at AEA Meeting

    • 1st Friday to Sunday of January
    • Typically 30 minute long
    • In a hotel room
    • Often sitting on a sofa
    • 1 to 8 interviewers
    • Last 5-10 min: their sales pitch (except top 20)

    8 Frequently Asked Questions

    • Tell us about your JMP (Always)
    • Grilling questions on JMP
    • Questions on other papers (sometimes)
    • What is your future research plan? (often)

    Your answers: same to all schools.

    8 Frequently Asked Questions (cont.)

    • What course(s) do you want to teach?
    • Are you willing to live in our city/town?
    • Which other schools are interviewing you?
    • Any questions about us?

    Your answers: customised to each school

    3. Flyouts

    Press < to go back to the previous section.

    Campus visit schedule

    • 9am: Arrive at campus
    • 30 min interview in a row
    • Lunch with faculty members
    • 30 min interview in a row
    • Maybe a 30 min break
    • 75-90 min job talk
    • 30 min interview in a row
    • Dinner with faculty members

    When a flyout offer comes...

    Leave a blank day between any two flyouts

    Call up schools (which you had an interview with) in the same area as the one offering you a flyout

    Job Talk Tips

    • Key for success: question-handling
    • Prepare intro to minimize irrelevant questions
    • Defer questions if you will answer them later
    • Finish on time

    What you can do NOW! #4

    Attend IIES job talks in January-February.

    Practice at IIES Brown Bag / Dept. Workshop.

    Present at conferences.

    30-min interviews

    • Department head: Explains job contract
    • Senior faculty: often challenges you
    • Junior faculty: often friendly, eager to know about you
    • 8 FAQs at AEA Meeting will be asked again
    • Ask YOUR questions
